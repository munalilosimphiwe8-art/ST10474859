Six-Month Programs

Our six-month programs are designed to provide deep, structured learning for individuals who want to build strong technical and life skills. These programs include:

  • Computer Literacy & Basic IT Skills
  • Entrepreneurship & Small Business Development
  • Childcare & Early Childhood Development
  • Gardening & Landscaping
  • Domestic Work Professional Skills

After completion, learners receive an accredited certificate and are prepared for both formal employment and self-employment.
